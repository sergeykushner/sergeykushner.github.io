Capsula - Digital Time Capsule

Save and lock text and images

Capsula is a unique app that lets users create and store digital capsules with memories, messages, and photos to open on a specified future date.

Key Features:

  • Create and customize digital capsules with text and images.
  • Set a future opening date for your capsules.
  • Secure storage with end-to-end encryption.
  • Sync your capsules across all your devices with iCloud.
  • User-friendly interface.

The app features seamless iCloud synchronization, allowing users to access their capsules across multiple devices signed in with the same Apple Account. Whether you want to preserve a special moment, send a message to your future self, or create a time capsule for loved ones, Capsula makes it easy and enjoyable.

With a strong emphasis on privacy and security, all user data is stored locally on the device and in iCloud using encryption, ensuring that your content remains private and secure.

Get Capsula today and start preserving your memories for the future!
